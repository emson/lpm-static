London Property Match
What area in London, does London Property Match search?
We currently operate in the South, Central and West areas of London.
What is the typical search?
There is no such thing. We help anyone looking for studio flats to large family homes.
How much do you charge?
We ask for an initial retainer fee to begin our search of£500 plus VAT. The fee is then calculated at the higher of£5,000 or 1% of the purchase price of the property plus VAT. The retainer fee is deducted from the final fee.
What does the retainer fee cover?
The retainer fee covers a four month search period. In the event that a client changes the brief during a search, or a suitable property is not found by us, we will retain the initial fee and have the right to charge additional reasonable expenses.
Why should we not go straight to the Estate Agents?
Estate agents work for and are paid by the seller, whereas we work exclusively for you the buyer.
