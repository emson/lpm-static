London Property Match

London Property Match is an independent property finding service that helps busy buyers find the right property, negotiate the best price and oversee the transaction through to completion.

Finding the right home or property investment in London can be time-consuming and stressful. We offer a bespoke service searching for, and advising on properties in Central, South and West London - saving buyers time, effort and, where possible, money.
