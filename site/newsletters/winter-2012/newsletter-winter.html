Newsletter | Winter 2012

Belated Happy New Year!

With Rightmove reporting stock levels being at their lowest in ten years and with a record number of house hunters registered, it will come as no surprise that good property is flying off the shelves and to secure it you will more than likely find yourself having to go to sealed bids. In January all the purchases we made on behalf of clients went to sealed bids. Amazingly we won four out of five of them.

In this issue we focus on Shepherd’s Bush, an area that perhaps is the last of the areas we search in central London that offers value for money. Savills talk us through their predictions for the property market over the next five years and as usual we summarise the best of what’s on in London this Winter.

Happy reading!
